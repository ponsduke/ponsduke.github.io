Meeting Duke RCR Policies

All Duke faculty and staff engaged in research must complete one online self-directed RCR 100-level course and one collaborative RCR 200-level course every three years.

  • If you have been recently identified as needing RCR training you may only be required to take EITHER a 100-level OR a 200-level course at this time. You can check your status on the RCR Tracker App.
  • Visit the RCR 100-level webpage and complete one of the multiple online self-directed course options as soon as possible.
  • Visit the RCR 200-level webpage to register for an upcoming live instructor-led event. Reviewing the events in advance increases the chances that you can find one of interest to you.
  • Visit our FAQ guide for additional information.
